Ponniyin Selvan (பொன்னியின் செல்வன், English: The Son of Ponni)
 is a historical novel by Kalki Krishnamurthy,
 written in Tamil. In five volumes, or about 2210 pages,
 it tells the story of early days of Arulmozhivarman (அருள்மொழிவர்மன்),
 who later became the great Chola emperor Rajaraja Chola I.
